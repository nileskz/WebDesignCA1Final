Report an Issue or Volunteer

This form can be used to report leaks, pollution or other water-related issues in the local area. It can also be used to sign up for clean-up events or workshops. All fields are required so that the message can be understood clearly.

Important Notes

For urgent emergencies, such as suspected contamination of a main public water supply, local emergency services should be contacted immediately.

This form is designed for non urgent updates, education events and general questions related to the SDG 6 project.
